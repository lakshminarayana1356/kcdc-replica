WELCOME TO KRISHNA CHAITANYA GROUP OF EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS

Krishna Chaitanya College has carved a niche for itself over a period of two decades. The milestone for Krishna Chaitanya College was laid by a two young men Dr.RV Krishna Reddy and P.Chandra Sekhar Reddy. In the Year 1998, Krishna Chaitanya College was started with three departments, 140 students and limited faculty in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. Since then it is constantly progressing in motivating students and staff engaged in active learning through the best use of modern technology. Krishna Chaitanya College is not just an educational institution, it is a commitment, towards building a new generation of smart and capable individuals. It not only provides quality Education, but teaches them the right attitude towards life. One among the top colleges in our state. Krishna Chaitanya College has wide reputation for holistic approach towards grooming the students. Today, Krishna Chaitanya happens to be one of the largest educational groups in the state of Andhra Pradesh with more than 300 faculty members and around 200 administrative staff and above 10000 students. Krishna Chaitanya is known for establishing unique traditions in every aspects of its functioning.
